MVP to Scale: Phased Delivery
Phase 1: Scoping & Business Case
Define problem, quantify impact, assess data, estimate resources, identify risks
Phase 2: MVP Development (3-6 months)
Functional prototype, simple data, essential features only, establish baselines
Phase 3: Pilot & Validation (2-4 months)
Limited rollout, gather production data, validate assumptions, assess readiness
Phase 4: Scale & Operations (6+ months)
Full deployment, production infrastructure, monitoring, retraining, governance audits
